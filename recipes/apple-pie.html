Apple Pie

Description

Because life isn't sweet enough

This simple but sweet delicacy originates from Rito Village where it was made using the succulent apples that grow in the surrounding
woodland. It's popularity quickly spread and it was even served at royal banquets at Hyrule Castle before the fall.

Ingredients

  • 1 Apple
  • 200g Tabantha Wheat
  • 100g Cane Sugar
  • 100g Goat Butter

Steps

  1. Grind the wheat into flour
  2. Mix the flour with the butter and some water to make the pastry
  3. Use the pastry to line a baking pot
  4. Chop up the apple into slices
  5. Heat up the cooking pot and add the apples
  6. Leave the apples to stew and add the sugar and stir
  7. Use the stewed apples to fill the pastry and cover with more pastry to make the pie
  8. Place the baking pot into the cooking pot and cover whilst it bakes
  9. Serve whilst still hot
