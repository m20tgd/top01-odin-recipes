Meat Stew

Description

A hearty meal for a hearty soul

The origin of this simple dish has been lost to the ages but what known for certain is that this simple dish is a favourite in every household in
the land. It's appeal is it's ability to fill any stomach whilst delighting the tongue with rich tastes with only simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Raw Meat
  • 200g Tabantha Wheat
  • 100g Goat Butter
  • 500ml Fresh Milk

Steps

  1. Dice the raw meat into suitable sized chunks
  2. Grind the wheat into a powder
  3. Heat up the cooking pot and add the butter
  4. Add the chunks to the cooking pot and sear until brown
  5. Add the milk to the cooking pot and stir in the wheat until it thickens
  6. Allow the stew to boil for 20 mins, stirring periodically
  7. Add any spices or herbs for taste
  8. Serve into bowls, allow to cool and enjoy!
