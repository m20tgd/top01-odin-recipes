Seafood Paella

Description

When lifes gives you fish and rice, make seafood paella

The dish of choice for beach dwellers and sailors of the open seas. Legend has it that this maritime feast was first created by a fishwife
from Lurelin village to celebrate her husband's return from the sea. It is still a popular recipe to this day and is a welcome sight.

Ingredients

  • 1 Mighty or Armoured Porgy
  • 1 Blueshell Snail
  • 200g Hylian Rice
  • 100g Goat Butter
  • 1tsp Rock Salt

Steps

  1. Fillet the porgy and chop into thick chunks
  2. Remove the snail from it's shell and slice into thin strips
  3. Heat up the cooking pot with a good size fire and add the goat butter
  4. Add the porgy and snail to the pan and stir into the melted butter
  5. Add the rice and let it cook until done
  6. Sprinkle in the rock salt and stir
  7. Spoon generous portions into bowls and serve
